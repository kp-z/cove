## 二、发现的不一致问题及修复

### 问题 1: Trigger 结构不一致

**Domain Layer** (`01-entity-layer.md`):
```yaml
triggers:
  - trigger_id: "trigger-001"
    trigger_type: "schedule"
    schedule:
      cron: "0 10 * * *"
```

**Application Layer 原始版本** (`02-runtime-layer.md`):
```yaml
trigger_manager:
  triggers:
    - trigger_id: "trigger-001"
      type: "schedule"  # ❌ 应为 trigger_type
      schedule: "0 10 * * *"  # ❌ 应为嵌套结构 {cron: "..."}
```

**修复**:
- 将 `type` 改为 `trigger_type`
- 将 `schedule: "..."` 改为 `schedule: {cron: "..."}`
- 修复位置: `02-runtime-layer.md` 第 1234 行、第 2567 行、`daemon.yaml` 示例

- 将扁平字符串数组改为对象数组 `{feature_id, permissions}`
